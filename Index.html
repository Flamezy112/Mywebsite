Simple scripts take anywhere from same day to 2 days. More advanced systems take 1 to 4 days depending on complexity. Full game development takes anywhere from 1 to 4 weeks depending on the scope and complexity of the project.

Reasonable revisions are included in all packages. Bug fixes are always free. Additional features beyond the original scope can be added for a fair additional cost.

I accept Crypto (preferred), PayPal, and Robux (though I prefer USD over Robux). For larger projects, I require 25-50% upfront and payment throughout tasks/milestones as they're completed.
