Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

Why should I hire a professional scripter? +
Professional scripters bring expertise, efficiency, and quality that saves you time and money in the long run. We write optimized, bug-free code that scales with your game, preventing costly rewrites later.
How long do scripts take to complete? +
Simple scripts take anywhere from same day to 2 days. More advanced systems take 1 to 4 days depending on complexity. Full game development takes anywhere from 1 to 4 weeks depending on the scope and complexity of the project.
What if I need changes after delivery? +
Reasonable revisions are included in all packages. Bug fixes are always free. Additional features beyond the original scope can be added for a fair additional cost.
What payment methods do you accept? +
I accept Crypto (preferred), PayPal, and Robux (though I prefer USD over Robux). For larger projects, I require 25-50% upfront and payment throughout tasks/milestones as they're completed.
What is your refund policy? +
I offer refunds if I cannot deliver on the agreed requirements. However, once work has begun and progress has been made, the deposit is non-refundable.
